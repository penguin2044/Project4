Sandwiches

Reuben

Our Reuben starts with tender, house-brined corned beef stacked high and layered with tangy sauerkraut, melted Swiss cheese, and a generous spread of our zesty Russian dressing—all grilled between slices of buttery marble rye until golden and toasty.

BLT

Our take on this timeless favorite starts with thick-cut, smoky bacon cooked to crispy perfection, layered with fresh romaine lettuce and juicy vine-ripened tomatoes. Nestled between slices of toasted sourdough and finished with a swipe of our house-made garlic mayo.

Club Sandwich

Our classic Club stacks oven-roasted turkey, crisp bacon, and smoked ham with fresh lettuce, ripe tomato, and sharp cheddar—all layered between three slices of toasted sourdough. Finished with a swipe of creamy mayo and a hint of mustard for that extra zing.

Ham & Cheese

Sliced smoked ham and mild cheddar cheese on your choice of bread, topped with crisp lettuce, tomato, and a swipe of mayo and mustard. Served cold or toasted—your call.

Turkey & Swiss

Oven-roasted turkey breast paired with Swiss cheese, lettuce, tomato, and red onion, finished with mayo on soft whole wheat bread. Light but loaded with flavor.

Egg Salad Sandwich

Our house-made egg salad is blended fresh daily with just the right balance of mayo, mustard, and seasoning, served on soft white or wheat bread with crisp romaine for a bit of crunch.
